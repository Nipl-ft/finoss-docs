# 1. Executive Summary

The AI Learning System is an intelligent decision-support module.

It analyzes market data, historical trades, strategies, and portfolio performance to provide insights that help improve trading decisions.

The AI does not directly execute trades.

---

# 2. Objectives

The AI system must:

- Analyze historical trades
- Identify trading patterns
- Detect recurring mistakes
- Summarize market conditions
- Evaluate strategies
- Assist with decision making
- Generate reports

# 4. AI Architecture

The AI module receives information from:

- Data Collection Engine
- Market Analysis Engine
- Trading Strategy Framework
- Risk Management System
- Paper Trading System
- Backtesting Engine
- Live Trading History

The AI combines these sources to generate recommendations and explanations.

---

# 5. Data Sources

The AI may analyze:

- OHLCV data

- Completed trades

# 7. AI Features

Version 1.0 includes:

- Market summaries
- Indicator explanations
- Strategy performance analysis
- Trade review
- Risk summaries
- Portfolio insights
- Daily reports

The AI explains *why* it reached its conclusions whenever possible.

---

# 8. Trade Review

After each completed trade, the AI evaluates:

- Entry quality
- Exit quality
- Risk/Reward ratio
- Position sizing
- Strategy compliance
- Market conditions

The goal is to help the trader learn and improve over time.

---

# 9. Strategy Analysis

The AI compares strategies using:

- Win rate
- Profit factor
- Drawdown
- Average return
- Trade frequency
- Market suitability

It highlights strengths and weaknesses rather than selecting a strategy automatically.

---

# 10. Risk Analysis

The AI reviews:

- Risk per trade
- Daily risk exposure
- Drawdown trends
- Consecutive losses
- Portfolio concentration

It can recommend adjustments but cannot override the Risk Management System.

---

# 11. Report Generation

Reports may include:

- Daily summary
- Weekly summary
- Monthly summary
- Strategy performance
- Trade review
- Portfolio performance
- Risk analysis

Reports should be viewable in the dashboard.

# 13. Performance

Goals:

- Fast response time
- Efficient data processing
- Reusable analysis
- Minimal impact on trading performance

---

# 14. Security

The AI:

- Cannot place trades
- Cannot access API secrets
- Uses validated data only
- Logs generated reports
- Respects user privacy
