# Executive Summary

**fin.oss** (Financial Operating System) is a personal AI-assisted cryptocurrency trading platform designed to automate and improve cryptocurrency trading on the Bybit exchange.

The platform combines market data collection, technical analysis, market condition detection, strategy execution, portfolio management, risk management, paper trading, backtesting, analytics, and future AI-assisted optimization into one integrated system.

The primary objective is not simply to automate trades, but to build a disciplined, data-driven trading environment that helps reduce emotional decision-making, improve consistency, and support continuous learning through recorded historical data and performance analysis.

This project is intended for **personal use** and is not designed as a commercial trading service.

---

# Background

Manual cryptocurrency trading requires continuous market monitoring, disciplined execution, and consistent risk management. Human emotions, fatigue, and delayed reactions can negatively affect trading performance.

Modern software technologies make it possible to automate repetitive tasks while maintaining consistent execution and detailed record keeping.

fin.oss aims to become a complete personal trading platform that combines automation with analytical tools and future AI capabilities.

---

# Vision Statement

To develop a secure, modular, intelligent, and reliable cryptocurrency trading platform that assists in making disciplined trading decisions through automation, structured data analysis, and continuous performance improvement.

---

# Mission Statement

The mission of fin.oss is to create a personal trading platform capable of:

- Collecting real-time market data.
- Monitoring cryptocurrency markets continuously.
- Applying rule-based trading strategies.
- Executing trades automatically.
- Managing trading risk consistently.
- Recording every trading activity.
- Evaluating trading performance.
- Supporting continuous improvement through historical analysis.
- Providing AI-assisted optimization in future versions.

---

# Problem Statement

Cryptocurrency markets operate continuously and change rapidly.

Manual trading presents several challenges:

- Emotional decision-making
- Missing trading opportunities
- Inconsistent execution
- Poor trade documentation
- Difficult performance evaluation
- Limited ability to monitor multiple assets simultaneously

fin.oss addresses these challenges by creating an automated and structured trading environment.

The development of fin.oss follows these principles:

# Design Philosophy

fin.oss is built around the philosophy that successful trading requires discipline, consistency, and measurable decision-making.

Automation should reduce emotional influence while maintaining transparency and accountability.

AI should initially assist by analyzing performance and suggesting improvements rather than replacing the core trading logic.
