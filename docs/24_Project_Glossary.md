# 2. General Terms

### Dashboard

The main user interface of fin.oss.

---

### Module

A self-contained part of the application responsible for a specific function.

---

### Service

A backend component responsible for performing a defined task.

---

### Workspace

A dedicated dashboard section such as Markets, Portfolio, or AI.

---

### Repository

The GitHub project containing the source code and documentation.

# 3. Trading Terms

### Position

An active trade that has been opened and not yet closed.

---

### Long Position

A trade that profits if the asset price increases.

---

### Short Position

A trade that profits if the asset price decreases.

---

### Leverage

Borrowed capital used to increase trading exposure.

---

### Stop Loss

An order designed to limit losses by closing a position at a predefined price.

---

### Take Profit

An order designed to lock in gains when a target price is reached.

---

### Risk/Reward Ratio

The relationship between potential profit and potential loss for a trade.

---

### Drawdown

The decline in account value from a peak to a subsequent low.

---

### PnL (Profit and Loss)

The financial result of a trade or portfolio.

---

# 4. Technical Analysis Terms

### OHLCV

Open, High, Low, Close, and Volume data for a market candle.

---

### EMA

Exponential Moving Average.

---

### SMA

Simple Moving Average.

---

### RSI

Relative Strength Index.

---

### MACD

Moving Average Convergence Divergence.

---

### ATR

Average True Range.

---

### Support

A price level where buying interest may increase.

---

### Resistance

A price level where selling interest may increase.

---

### Trend

The general direction of market movement.

---

# 5. AI Terms

### AI Insight

A generated observation based on market or trading data.

---

### Trade Review

An AI-generated evaluation of a completed trade.

---

### Strategy Review

An AI-generated evaluation of strategy performance.

---

### Prompt

Instructions provided to an AI model to generate a response.

---

### Confidence Score

A numerical estimate of confidence associated with an AI-generated insight or trading signal.

---

# 6. Database Terms

### Table

A structured collection of related records.

---

### Row

A single record within a table.

---

### Column

A field within a table.

---

### Primary Key

A unique identifier for each row.

---

### Foreign Key

A reference to a related row in another table.

---

### Row-Level Security (RLS)

Database policies that restrict access to rows based on the authenticated user.

---

# 7. API Terms

### Endpoint

A URL exposed by the backend to perform a specific operation.

---

### Request

Data sent from the client to the server.

---

### Response

Data returned by the server to the client.

---

### JWT

JSON Web Token used for authentication.

---

### REST API

An API that follows Representational State Transfer principles.

---

### WebSocket

A persistent communication channel that supports real-time data exchange.

---

# 8. Security Terms

### Authentication

Verifying the identity of a user.

---

### Authorization

Determining what an authenticated user is allowed to do.

---

### Encryption

Protecting data by converting it into an unreadable format.

---

### Audit Log

A permanent record of important system events.

---

### Least Privilege

Granting only the permissions required to perform a task.

---

# 9. Project-Specific Terms

### fin.oss

The AI-assisted cryptocurrency trading platform described by this documentation.

---

### Strategy Framework

The module responsible for generating trading signals.

---

### Risk Management System

The module that validates all trading decisions before execution.

---

### Order Execution Engine

The module responsible for submitting approved orders to the exchange.

---

### Paper Trading

A simulated trading environment using live market data without real funds.

---

### Backtesting

Evaluating strategies using historical market data.

---

### AI Learning System

The module that analyzes trading activity and generates insights and reports.
